Cardio

Our fitness center holds over 120 pieces of cardiovascular equipment, including Precor upright bikes, recumbent bikes, treadmills, ellipticals and adaptive motion trainers. You can create a Preva account to track your workouts over time. In addition, we offer rowers, a Jacobs Ladder and Assault Air bikes for those who love interval and high intensity training.

Strength Training

Are you looking for resistance training? We have all the right equipment for you! Our fitness center offers dumbbells ranging from 3 to 125 lbs., eight power racks, multiple air-powered Kieser cable machines, a plate circuit, a machine circuit and a functional Queenex frame complete with TRX suspension trainers, battle ropes and kettlebells.

Yellow Breeches

The Yellow Breeches are an icon here at Messiah College. You will often see students tubing down the breeches or splashing their feet in the water to cool off on a warm day.

Fredericksen Natatorium

Located on the ground floor of Sollenberger Sports Center, and home to Messiah’s swim teams, the Fredricksen Natatorium includes eight 25-yard lanes and updated bleacher seating.

Tennis Courts

Criste Courts are six match-quality tennis courts (hard), plus four additional recreational courts. While providing the student body and community with a recreational tennis venue, the courts are also home to the Falcons’ men’s and women’s tennis teams.

Fitness trail

With its picturesque setting, mulched pathways, and frequently spotted wildlife, the Stabler Fitness Trail gives you the perfect setting for a serene and peaceful workout.

Outdoor track

The Starry outdoor track, surfaced with cushion, is used for recreation and sports team such as track and field.

Group fitness

Do you enjoy taking group fitness classes? With our certified and well-experienced instructors, you will be challenged and motivated to work out in a group setting. Varying on the semester, you have the option to take classes such as spin, core class, pilates, gentle yoga, active yoga, no-machine resistance training and aquatics.

Jordan Court

The newly-renovated arena, which hosts our basketball and volleyball games, seats more than 1,600 fans in the facility and holds a hanging scoreboard, as well as a state-of-the-art videoboard.
